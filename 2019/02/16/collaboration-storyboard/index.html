    Discuss: choose a topic

    In my expectation, Love and War would be the topic in the communication. And I want to use Two story at first, then they will concentrate. It will show the influence of war.

    When we discuss this idea, my collaborator said, the reason of war is hard to be expressed by communication. In my view, conflict and war could be caused by false communication, so the communication in love and war would have common point. But, the reason of war could be influenced by many aspects, not only communication.

    Furthermore, in this project, we have not too much time, so we should limit the quantity of work. If we talk about war, we will use more time on contrast / influence between two points, that would be complicated, and easy to make audience be confused.

    So we will talk about Love only in animated illustration
