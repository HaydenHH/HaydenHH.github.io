    History of conceptual art

    What is art, why it can be an art. Who define it is art. There are many problem we should think about. These conceptual artist both create some innovative / creative even crazy thing, they ask public, what is art.

    Paul Cezanne - trees and Rocks near the chateau Noir

    For expressionism, a painting could represent nothing, it show visuals. For conceptual art, Duchamp created Fountain which is a signatured toilet put in a gallery, he changed people’s attitude to art.

    Robert Rauschenburg, eraesd.
    Is painting be valued by the aritist’s popularity?

    Chirs Burden, shoot

    Michael Craig-Martin,an Oak tree

    The work shows a glass desk which put a cup of water, which shows affordance to locate / put somehing there. But the desk is so high so people are impossible to put thing on there, which shows hindrance

    Marina Abramovic, Rhythm 0

    The artist allow audience see herself as a object, and use some tool on her body, base on please or punish. People hurt her in the end. This performance show human’s evil heart

    Richard Prince, cowboy

    Damien Hirst, A Thousand Years

    An installation which division death and live.

    Tracey Emin, My bed

    Arst’s show a bed which audience can imagine the situation of the holder. It shows some negative / despair, so it has a potential narrative ability.

    Cornelia Parker, the Distance

    Andrea Fraser, untitled

    Fraser problematizes whether selling art to collectors in of itself is a form of prostitution.

    Mediengruppe Bitnik, Surveillance Chess

    Who can read the art

    The reason of a creation of a art, must be understood. whatever it can be or not, art should be read. Who is the audience? What kind of people have the ability to read? It is a scope of art. Which means, this thing in these crowds, would be seen as art, and in others people’s opinion, it could be nothing even a rubbish. So when we talk about an art, or discuss is it an art, shall we think about the context of creation, reason of creation.

    Art without audience

    If an art which doesn’t need any audience, the artist do the work only for self, it show his own feeling, emotion, is it an Art? If you understand the meaning of that, or you appreciate the visuals of that, you think it is a art; if not, that is not art for you. So for an art, a personal art, the value not be defined as how much people understand that, is could be how it shows author’s idea.

    Are art works as common goods

    We know arts can take commercial profit for artist, even Van Gogh sold his work for survive as well. So, art definitely can be a kind of good, cause it bring money.
