    collaboration with Film maker
     journal

    Author:Hayden

    collaboration with a Film maker

    To complete the project which require collaboration with other, I found a student who learning in MA Film. She has professional cognition in shot design, so I can get advanced suggestion on animation making or screen writing.

    The early topic we chose is about relationship in contemporary partner.

    Because of the culture shock, emotion between different people could be hard to understand by other who from different countries. Therefore we are going to focus more on behaviour side, which means, in the animation, the emotion expression could not be rendered as main point, the phenomenon and behaviour will be more important in this project.

    Reason I explore relationship between partner

    There are many developed countries are holding a low population growth, evermore negative growth. In my opinion, this phenomenon is because of the change of marry. Children and children education is becoming kind of bear in a family especially in high-knowledge level group. More and more partner have strong individual concept, and they are becoming more independent.
    So I want to explore the reason people become more independent with partner, it could be include the physical factors or emotional reason.
