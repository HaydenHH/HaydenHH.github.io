    Trip to tate

    In this Friday, we went to the Tate modern museum to catch inspiration. Actually, I visited here twice, but this time is still gave me new ideas even something I didn’t understand.

    atmosphere of building

    As a famous contemporary museum, Tate modern has a good design at interior design and architecture. We can see a clear structure in this building, and wall paint, sometimes the wall is original so it shows a concrete material or iron. In my view, it can decrease the attraction of building so viewer can focus more on exhibitions.

    A big painting on ground

    I don’t know who is the man painted on ground XD, and I even don’t know it is a painting before I backed to upper floor. It is really big so people stand beside the painting are hard to fully view it. To more specific observe, it is paint as different points with different distance and size, it could be generated by Python or other programme. In the picture I took, there is a child is standing in the work, she might not know she has been surrounded by this art work, but this work indeed effected her. Is it the effect of art? It could be invisible or potential, but it does work.

    As well as atmosphere/ environment, it will not illustrate a specific meaning, but it effect on content and feeling.

    Light and Dark

    The Light & Dark always can be the good tool for artists. It create strong visual to catch focus.

    The motional text with bright light can easily get attention, and the artist used angle and bulb to create a triangle light.

    And in this work,

    light generated sort of shadow which have different clarity, so it exhibit the change of hierarchy because of the distance between objects and light source.

    Magic of materials

    Contemporary artist generally will not be limited by materials, they can show concept through any thing if it is possible.

    I saw the upper one as a piece of soil which be lifted, so it could be understood as the remove of soil, and the conflict between nature and human.

    Materials can take directly feeling which can guide viewer to associate with relative expression. Furthermore, it can combine with graphic language to improve visual effect.

    history of art

    After trip in Tate, I joint a lecture which speak by Ben. He briefly talked about history of art. As a designer, I must admit I have not enough interests in fine arts and classical art. But I do like expressionism or abstractionism.

    the progress of art

    Does idea being more important in art? Review the history of art, ideas and concepts look take more parts in an art work, some artist even gave traditional methods up to show their idea.

    As a part of culture, art can be seen as the tool which convey ideology and attitude, so public require art become more expressive, especially during a war.

    Does art influence a War

    The art work after war could be more popular for a collector, cause we can clear see the influence of war to the artist or society. But, can we find some work which effect a war? We see, some style such as Dada / Pop art, are influenced by war, they could be the result of war.

    Futurism was popular in early 20th Centry Italy, it focus on expression of speed/ motion/ machine/ industry, in my view, it promote the increase of materialism in Italy.

    Reproduction in famous arts
     journal

    Author:Hayden

    2019-01-14

    reproduction

    Today’s manufacture will copy or reproduce art work in a bad quality or massive quantity. It improve the influence of some great art works, but there are some negative influences in time:

    1. overexposure
    2. decrease potential value because of the massive copies
    3. change the attitude of visitor For more example of reproduction, many of art works have been displayed on Web, App, sorts of media and screen.

    required commercial value

    Museum or gallery announce them through reproduce classical works, it indeed in work, attract more viewer / gain more profit in sub-production. So they can have more opportunities
    to protect arts.

    infinite reproduce, infinite misunderstanding

    As a significant count of reproduction have been made in market, audiences have a high cognition to some classical art work. In the time, they will have different attitude to these sub-production as well as paintings. In today’s workshop, my peers created some experiments to express the process of misunderstanding and distortion.

    repeat photocopy

    Photocopy a painting with 10 times, what does it look like? Digitally, pixel will increase, so it will lose details and sharpness. Furthermore, the colours will change to be more bright which have higher saturation. Fine lines will become bolder and look as if they’ve been engrave , because of lines look like rise up, and generate bigger contrast with background, so it has an engrave-alike texture.

    de-adjective

    People generally describe and understand object through read adjective. A peer removed most adjectives in a text which describe the girl with pearl earring. Then she found it look as if it is describing Mona Lisa. It shows the influence of second-hand cognition, people will easily change the attitude because of different descriptions. So I thought we can see something in different angle, a general sight and illustratively.
